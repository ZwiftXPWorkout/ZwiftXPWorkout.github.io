What Is This?

This app creates a steady-state Zwift workout featuring 61-second intervals, each earning you 12 XP.

Why Use It?

In Zwift, XP is awarded only after earning a star. If you end a workout early, you might miss out on earned XP. This app minimizes that risk.

How It Helps

Short 61-second intervals ensure you collect XP consistently, allowing you to stop your workout at pretty much any time without losing XP.

When To Use It

This workout earns you 12 XP every 61 seconds, translating to 708 XP per hour, equivalent to riding at 35 km/h. You can ride at a lower intensity and still maximize your XP gain.
